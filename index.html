Hello

I was born and raised in New York. I'm grateful for where I am.

I've got an interesting background: from the age of 12, I more or less raised myself. In 2007 my parents had left the country. Around the age of 14, up untill the end of high school, I was homeless. Around the same time I started getting interested in Technology. Through ambition, I was able to gradaute from High School and attend college to complete 74 credits towards both a Computer Science and Engineering Science Degree. Eventually, I realized I couldn't financially keep attending college.

Taking what I learned from building custom computers, tinkering with electronics and writing code, I landed myself a job in IT. Four years later, I was recruited by Microsoft. and helped them open their first Flagship store in 5th Ave, Manhattan. I realized I was no longer writing code and decided to pursue my passion as a developer.

Now I turn ideas into reality through code. I specialize in React + Redux front-end, Rails back-end and Postgresql database for web development. When I’m not working, you can find me viewing React Native tutorials, scrolling through Dribbble for UI/UX inspiration or watching Rick and Morty on Adult Swim.
